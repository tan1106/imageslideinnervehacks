Round 01

-A technical test will be held online on the Coding Ninjas platform.
- The syllabus includes Data Structures and Algorithms, Competitive Coding Problems, and objective questions on C++, Java, Python, HTML, CSS, etc.
- All registered teams need to participate and the average of the team will be calculated

Round 02


- Shortlisted teams will be divided among 5 problem statements and be expected to develop a web/app/prototype of their solution.

- 2 evaluations will be done by Hackathon mentors for shortlisting teams on the basis of their progress.

Round 03


- The top 50 teams will be shortlisted for the final round on 1st October 2022

- Teams will have to report offline to the IGDTUW campus for the presentation round
